# Approval Workflow

## Submission
Status = Pending

## Approval
Status = Approved

## Rejection
Status = Rejected

## Benefits
- Controlled approval process
- Better tracking
- Improved accountability

---

# Workflow

1. Employee logs in.
2. Employee submits leave request.
3. Request status becomes Pending.
4. Approval process starts.
5. Administrator reviews request.
6. Administrator approves or rejects.
7. Status is updated.
8. Reports and dashboards are refreshed.
