# Task Answers – Day 19

## What is Agentforce?

Agentforce is Salesforce's AI platform that allows organizations to build intelligent agents capable of understanding requests and performing business actions automatically.

---

## How are AI Agents different from Chatbots?

Chatbots mainly answer questions.

AI Agents can:
- Answer questions
- Access business data
- Trigger workflows
- Perform actions
- Support decision-making

---

## Why is AI becoming important in enterprise systems?

AI helps organizations:
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Improve efficiency
- Analyze data quickly
- Enhance user experience
- Reduce operational costs

---

## How can AI improve business processes?

AI can:
- Recommend actions
- Automate support
- Analyze information
- Generate insights
- Improve productivity

---

## Why should enterprises control AI systems?

Without proper controls, AI may:
- Produce incorrect results
- Access sensitive data
- Perform unintended actions
- Create compliance issues

Therefore governance and monitoring are important.
