## 1️⃣ Why Enterprise Systems Need Modular Architecture

Modular architecture improves:
- Scalability
- Reusability
- Maintainability
- Team collaboration

---

## 2️⃣ Why Relationships Are Important

Relationships connect business data and maintain proper data organization.

---

## 3️⃣ Why Flows Are Insufficient for Some Cases

Complex logic, integrations, and large data processing require Apex programming.

---

## 4️⃣ Why Systems Need Event-Driven Behavior

Systems must react automatically to real-time business events and updates.

---

## 5️⃣ Why UI/Backend Separation Is Important

Separation improves:
- Security
- Maintainability
- Scalability
- Performance

---

## 6️⃣ Why Enterprise Systems Require Testing

Testing prevents bugs and ensures system reliability.

---

## 7️⃣ Why Reusable UI Architecture Is Powerful

Reusable components reduce duplicate work and improve maintainability.

---

## 8️⃣ Problems When Systems Scale

- Performance issues
- Security risks
- Database load
- Notification overload

---

## 9️⃣ Why Automation Should Be Designed Carefully

Poor automation can create incorrect workflows and system errors.
