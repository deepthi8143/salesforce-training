Day 11 Answers

## 1️⃣ Why Testing Matters

Testing prevents bugs and improves enterprise software reliability.

---

## 2️⃣ What is Asynchronous Processing?

Async processing executes tasks in the background instead of immediately.

---

## 3️⃣ Important Test Cases

- Invalid email
- Duplicate registration
- Seats exceeding limit
- Attendance validation
- Trigger execution
- Notification delivery
- Database update handling
- Security checks
- Fee calculation
- Required field validation

---

## 4️⃣ Async Use Cases

- Bulk emails
- Report generation
- Data synchronization
- Notifications
- Large imports

---

## 5️⃣ Reliability Discussion

If systems fail during operations:
- Data inconsistency may occur
- Notifications may fail
- Business operations may stop

Testing helps identify these issues early.

---

## 6️⃣ Reflection

Enterprise systems require testing and async processing because they handle:
- Large data
- Multiple users
- Heavy automation
- Real-time business workflows.
