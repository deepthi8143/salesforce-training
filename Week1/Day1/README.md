# 📚 Topics Learned

## ✅ Introduction to Salesforce
- Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform
- Used by companies to manage customers and business operations
- Helps improve productivity and customer relationships

---

## ✅ What is CRM?
- CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management
- Helps businesses manage customer interactions
- Stores customer data in one place
- Improves communication and support services

---

## ✅ Salesforce Interface Basics
- App Launcher
- Navigation Bar
- Leads and Contacts
- Accounts and Opportunities
- Reports and Dashboards

---

## ✅ Salesforce Objects
- Objects are database tables in Salesforce
- Used to store information

### Examples:
- Account
- Contact
- Lead
- Opportunity

---

## ✅ Records and Fields
- Records are single entries inside objects
- Fields store specific information

### Example:
| Name | Phone |
|---|---|
| Rahul | 9876543210 |

---

## ✅ Types of Objects

### Standard Objects
Objects already available in Salesforce.

Examples:
- Account
- Contact
- Lead

### Custom Objects
Objects created according to business requirements.

Examples:
- Student Object
- Employee Object

---

## ✅ Salesforce Admin vs Developer

| Salesforce Admin | Salesforce Developer |
|---|---|
| Configures Salesforce | Develops applications |
| Uses settings and tools | Writes Apex code |
| Manages users | Creates automation |
