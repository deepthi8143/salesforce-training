# 📘 Learnings – Day 1

Today I learned many important Salesforce concepts.

## Key Learnings

- Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.
- CRM helps companies manage customer relationships.
- Objects are database tables in Salesforce.
- Records are individual entries inside objects.
- Salesforce provides Standard and Custom Objects.
- Salesforce Developers build applications and automation.
- Salesforce Admins configure and manage the platform.
- Salesforce is widely used in real-world business applications.
