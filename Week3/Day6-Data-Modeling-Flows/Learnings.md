# ✅ Key Learnings

- Salesforce data models help organize and manage business data efficiently.
- Custom objects and fields can be created to meet specific business requirements.
- Master-Detail and Lookup relationships define how records are connected.
- Record-Triggered Flows automate processes without writing code.
- Flow Builder provides a powerful declarative tool for business automation.

# 🚀 Real-World Understanding

I learned how businesses use Salesforce data models to store and manage information in a structured way. I also learned how Record-Triggered Flows can automate tasks such as updating records, sending notifications, and executing business processes automatically when data changes.

# 📌 Most Interesting Topic

Record-Triggered Flows were the most interesting topic because they enable powerful automation without requiring code. Building flows to respond automatically to record changes demonstrates how Salesforce can streamline business operations efficiently.
