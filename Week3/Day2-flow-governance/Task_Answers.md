Task Answers – Day 14

# 1️. Why can’t enterprise systems allow everyone to directly change important records?

Because it may lead to:
- security issues
- misuse of data
- incorrect approvals
- business risks
- inconsistent records

Controlled access improves system reliability.

---

# 2️. Explain branching workflow logic

Example:

If attendance < 75%
→ warning email

If attendance < 60%
→ parent notification

If attendance < 50%
→ admin escalation

Different actions happen based on decision conditions.

---

# 3️. Why do enterprises require controlled workflows?

Controlled workflows:
- improve security
- maintain accountability
- prevent unauthorized changes
- support business governance
- improve operational consistency

---

# 4️. Why are approval workflows useful?

Approval workflows help:
- manage sensitive operations
- ensure proper authorization
- improve transparency
- maintain business control
