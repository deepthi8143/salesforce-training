 Task Answers – Day 15

# 1️⃣ Why is clean and reliable data important?

Enterprise systems use data for:
- reports
- automation
- notifications
- analytics
- decision making

Incorrect data affects the entire system.

---

# 2️⃣ Problems caused by duplicate records

Duplicate records may cause:
- repeated notifications
- incorrect reports
- fee issues
- attendance confusion
- inaccurate dashboards

---

# 3️⃣ Migration challenges from Excel to Salesforce

Challenges include:
- duplicate records
- missing data
- invalid formats
- inconsistent information
- incorrect field mapping

---

# 4️⃣ Problems if 50,000 records are imported incorrectly

Possible issues:
- wrong reports
- automation failures
- incorrect analytics
- notification errors
- system inconsistency
