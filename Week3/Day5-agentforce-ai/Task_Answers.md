# 1. AI Agent Use Cases

## College Management

### AI Attendance Assistant

- Tracks attendance automatically.
- Alerts students about low attendance.

### AI Course Advisor

- Suggests courses based on student interests.
- Recommends learning paths.

---

## Placements

### AI Placement Recommendation System

- Matches students with suitable job opportunities.
- Suggests required skills for target roles.

---

## Recruitment

### AI Resume Screening Assistant

- Reviews applications.
- Shortlists eligible candidates automatically.

---

## Student Support

### AI Helpdesk Assistant

- Answers student questions instantly.
- Provides academic information.

---

## Faculty Operations

### AI Scheduling Assistant

- Helps manage classes and meetings.

# 2. AI Workflow Thinking

User asks a question

↓

AI Agent receives the request

↓

Agent analyzes the query

↓

Flow or Apex executes required business logic

↓

Database retrieves or updates records

↓

AI generates a response

↓

Action is executed automatically

↓

User receives the result

This workflow allows AI agents to combine intelligence with enterprise business processes.

---

# 3. Risks of AI Systems

## Hallucinations

AI may generate incorrect information.

## Wrong Automation

Incorrect actions may affect business processes.

## Privacy Risks

Sensitive information may be exposed.

## Bias

AI may produce unfair recommendations.

## Incorrect Approvals

AI decisions may not always be accurate.

## Over-Automation

Too much automation may reduce human oversight.

---

# 4. Reflection

AI agents will significantly change enterprise software development by automating repetitive tasks, improving decision-making, and providing intelligent assistance.
In the future, developers may spend more time designing AI workflows and governance policies rather than building every process manually.
