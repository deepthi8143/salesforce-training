# 🤖 What is Agentforce?

Agentforce is Salesforce's AI platform that enables organizations to build intelligent AI agents capable of understanding requests, accessing business data, and performing actions automatically.

Unlike traditional chatbots, Agentforce agents can reason, take actions, interact with business processes, and support users in real-world scenarios.

---

# 🌟 Why Agentforce is Important

Businesses generate huge amounts of data every day.

AI agents help organizations by:

- Automating repetitive tasks
- Reducing manual effort
- Improving customer experience
- Increasing productivity
- Providing intelligent recommendations
- Supporting employees and customers
